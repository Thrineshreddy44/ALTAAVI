Who We Are?

FujSeng Construction LLC, was established in the year 2006 as a Contracting Company, licensed in “Unlimited Category” in the Emirates of Fujairah and has over the period expanded its operations in the Emirates of Abu Dhabi, Dubai and Ras al Khaimah as well.
Fujseng, over the period, with its well planned backward integration program, has now emerged as a self-contained, multi-disciplined complete Engineering Company (also known as Fujairah Engineering, FujEng), capable of undertaking E.P.C / Design & Build Contracts.

Our Vision & Mission

Our vision & mission, is built on the principles of innovation and to maintain / continually strengthen our Core Engineering Expertise.

Our strategy for sustained growth is anchored in the development of world-class constructions/developments, our far-reaching national network and its financing capabilities.

Our Motto
